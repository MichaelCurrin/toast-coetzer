Here are some bits and pieces written between 2 January and 20 April 2013. I will update this section from time to time. The idea is that you can print out the whole batch and go read it somewhere nice. Engels/ Afrikaans.

Some are titled, others not. Some have/will become songs for The Buckfever Underground, others for Simply Dead. Just nod and pretend if you're not with me here.

The lyrics for all the latest Buckfever albums are also available to read online on the Buckfever [Music](https://www.thebuckfeverunderground.co.za/music/) page - find an album and click on one of the songs titles under the _Tracklist / Lyrics_ section. Here is our latest album: [The Last Days of Beautiful](https://www.thebuckfeverunderground.co.za/music/last_days_of_beautiful/).

Thanks, enjoy!
